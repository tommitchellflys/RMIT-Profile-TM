IT Project
  • IT Project.

    Your Sis, My Bro, Our Family! – All inclusive chat help app, For troubled people suffering from depression due to Covid.

    Overview: The project will be a peer to peer chat style service provided, via a dedicated website / smart phone application. It is designed to help negate / battle the effects of the global pandemic.

    Motivation: As we are all aware, Covid 19 has swept the world, people are isolated, losing their jobs, homes, businesses, right to travel, access to their families often this is just the beginning. In 2020 60,000+ Australians and countless other nations natives also were stranded overseas without the ability to come home. I myself was one of these Australians, often stranded nationals find themselves stranded in countries where the native tongue is not their own, as one of these victims of the global pandemic I know firsthand how important it can be to just have someone to talk too, someone who you can just be yourself too and gain a grip of your reality.

    Description: My project Idea is to build a smart phone app/web service where people can go – find a like-minded person who is in a position to sacrifice a small bit of time to listening and forming a relationship with people. The goal being simply people being there for other people. In order to help combat the resulting depression / suicide coming from this pandemic. The product would simply be a chat service. Upon registering for the app, you would put in your name, age and nationality. The app would then match you up with a * Your Sis, My Bro* advisor who suits your age and language requirements resulting in the user having someone to talk too. After 3 successful “chats” the user would be prompted to undergo a quick training on the app to become a *Your sis, My bro* advisor. Advisor training would be simple but effective. Although advisor training would only be 5 minutes it would go over key insights on how to be an effective listener and also on how to identify a potentially harmful situation. The app would include inbuilt functionality for contacting emergency services for the user’s current location in the world and simple prompts in the case that the advisor could not communicate in the emergency services language depending on the location of the user. Through sponsorship with airlines, supermarkets clothing stores, advisors would be able to redeem points earned for each relationship / conversation and 5-star rating for real world helpful items / services to help incentivise advisors and ensure a high-quality product is delivered.

    Tools: Html to build the website. JavaScript, CSS for styling. Java to build the mobile app including hybrid chat system to allow Browser, Android and IOS synchronicity. Cloud storage – IBM offers secure commercial solutions Laptops. Potential for open-source collaboration via GITHUB Advertisement solutions – Google, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, SEO, Blogging, Government initiatives, sponsorships with relative brands.

    Skills required: Front end development-html, CSS, JavaScript Backend development- Java/JavaScript Mobile app development (both android and IOS) Java / JavaScript Gui development advisor Project management Lawyer Financial advisor Marketing professional The skills and resources to put together a project like this is definitely doable with outsourcing options such as fiverr , Upwork etc. I believe this project is completely doable and would be an extremely beneficial product once launched. The main problem I see for a project of this type is the lack of monetization. This project would mainly be a passion project with limited possibilities for monetization, possibly the website and application could generate revenue from add space if it as to take off.

    Outcome: I believe that if this project was to come to reality it would have a real benefit for a lot of people world-wide. It would easily be able to shift focus from Covid once the pandemic has come to an end and quickly shift focus onto another area such men and women with mental health issue, troubled teens etc. all in all I think a free place to go and chat when you need someone to talk to would be extremely welcomed and with the right level of community support would be welcomed worldwide!
